About ARYA UDAY

aryauday is a multifaceted NGO dedicated to holistic social and ethical transformation. The organization operates through structured wings that address education, youth development, elderly care, cultural preservation, women’s safety, and Gau-Raksha initiatives. commanded by founder of aryauday (yashvardhan shrivastava)

The NGO believes in discipline, coordinated action, and measurable impact.

Our Core Wing – ARYA UDAY

Structured initiatives for education, youth, elderly care, and culture.

Free Child Education

  • Free study classes for underprivileged children
  • Support for students lacking digital access
  • Academic mentoring and foundational development

Sports Promotion

  • Youth engagement through sports
  • Physical fitness and discipline development

Support for Elderly (Vridh Ashrams)

  • Assistance to senior citizens
  • Emotional and social support initiatives

Cultural & Temple Development

  • Cultural preservation programs
  • Temple construction and restoration initiatives

Specialized Wing – PRATYANGIRA NARSIMHA VAHINI

प्र न व (PNV)

PRATYANGIRA NARSIMHA VAHINI (PNV) is the specialized operational wing of ARYA UDAY working in active coordination with law enforcement authorities. This wing focuses on decisive action in matters of Women’s Safety and Gau-Raksha.
